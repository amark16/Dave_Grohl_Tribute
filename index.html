Dave Grohl

Rising from the ashes of Nirvana to create one of the most popular acts in modern Rock music

A timeline of Dave Grohl

  • 1969: Born on January 14th in Warren, Ohio.
  • 1981: Began learning to play the guitar by himself.
  • 1982: Attended his first concert (Naked Raygun) at age 13 at the Cubby Bear in Chicago.
  • 1985: Joined band Dain Bramage, after stints with various other local bands.
  • 1987: Grohl leaves Dain Bramage to join Scream.
  • 1990: Scream disbands suddenly and Grohl is looking for a new gig.
  • 1990: Dave Grohl auditions for Nirvana and joins Kurt Cobain and Krist Novoselic.
  • 1994: In April, Nirvana comes to an abrupt end following the death of Kurt Cobain.
  • 1994: After a brief periof of introspection, Dave Grohl enters the studio in October and records 15 songs all by himself under the pseudonym "Foo Fighters".
  • 1995: The Foo Fighters release their self-title debut album, followed by two decades of creating rock n' roll history
  • 2014: Nirvana is inducted into the Rock 'n Roll Hall of Fame
  • 2020: The Foo Fighters celebrate their 25th anniversary.
  • 2021: The Foo Fighters released their latest album "Medicine at Midnight".
